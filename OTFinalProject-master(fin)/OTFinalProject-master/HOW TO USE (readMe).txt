HOW TO USE:

Register an account first

Log in

To reserve your movie:
	Enter a quantity below each poster
	Click add to cart

To View your reserved tickets:
	Click "Refresh Cart" button

To update your ticketL
	Click the item ON YOUR 'RESERVED TICKETS' section.
	a pop up will show
	enter the quantity you want to update
	press "update" button
	then view your cart

To delete your reserved ticket
	Click the item ON YOUR 'RESERVED TICKETS' section.
	a pop up will show
	press "delete" button
	then view your cart
	
To purchase tickets:
	Click "purchase" button
	enter a 9 digit credit card number
	Press "purchase"

To view History:
	Click "History button"
